1. You have to guess a five letter word that will be randomly assigned.
2. There are three cases after you enter your guess:
    (A) The word is correctly guessed: ALL LETTERS GREEN
    (B) The letters are correct but wrongly placed LETTERS ARE YELLOW
    (C) None of the letters are correct LETTERS ARE GRAY
3. You have 10 chances to guess the word
4. You final score will be calculated as follows:
                Final Score = (Number of chances taken) * 100
